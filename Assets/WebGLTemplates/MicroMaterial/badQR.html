Scanning random QR codes can be dangerous!

You just scanned a QR code, and while this one is safe, not all QR codes are. A malicious QR code can redirect you to harmful websites, steal personal data, or even install malware on your device.

Attackers can:

  • Redirect you to phishing websites to steal your passwords or sensitive information.
  • Send you to malicious sites that install malware on your device.
  • Display fake login screens that capture your credentials.
  • Trigger unwanted actions, like sending messages without your consent.
